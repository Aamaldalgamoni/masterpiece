About Sunshine Academy:

At Little Explorer Preschool, we provide an engaging, safe, and stimulating environment for young learners. Our experienced educators focus on nurturing curiosity, problem-solving skills, and a love for learning through exploration-based activities. We offer a well-rounded education that includes early literacy, numeracy, social-emotional learning, and physical development. Our preschool provides opportunities for children to engage with the world around them, developing essential skills through structured play and interactive activities. We ensure that each child is supported in their personal growth, helping them build self-confidence, creativity, and positive relationships with peers and adults.

Facilities & Environment:

Classrooms: Bright, spacious rooms equipped with educational tools, books, and creative learning stations. Outdoor Play Area: A secure and safe playground for physical activities such as climbing, running, and group games. Art Room: A creative space for crafting, painting, and hands-on artistic expression. Cafeteria: Offers nutritious snacks and lunch options, or children may bring their own meals. Rest Areas: Comfortable areas for quiet time or napping for younger children. Safety Measures: Secure entrances, CCTV monitoring, and fully-trained staff to ensure a safe environment.

Staff & Qualifications:

Experienced Educators: All teachers at Little Explorer Preschool are qualified in early childhood education with expertise in child development and play-based learning. Teacher-Child Ratio: We maintain a low ratio to ensure personalized attention and quality care. Our ratio is 1 teacher per 8 children in the preschool programs and 1 teacher per 4 children in the toddler programs. Support Staff: We have a team of support staff, including a child psychologist and learning assistants, who are available to help address any specific needs..
